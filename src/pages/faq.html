FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bookify?

Bookify is an online platform where you can discover, read, and share books with a passionate community of readers.

How can I sign up?

You can sign up by clicking the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage and filling out the registration form.

Is Bookify free?

Yes, Bookify offers a free membership with the option to upgrade to premium plans for additional features.

Can I add my own books?

Absolutely! You can add books to your collection and share them with other users on the platform.

How do I contact customer support?

If you have any issues or questions, you can reach our support team via the 'Contact Us' page.
